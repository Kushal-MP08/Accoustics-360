What is acoustic spray?

A seamless, spray-applied cellulose coating that controls reverberation while preserving exposed decks, beams, services and architectural intent.

Applied directly to concrete, metal deck, gypsum or prepared existing substrates, the system creates a continuous sound-absorptive layer with minimal visual interruption.
